WebBudots

Installation, June 2025

WebBudots is a web-based tribute to Budots, a grassroots electronic dance genre that originated from Davao City, Philippines. DJ Love (also known as Sherwin Tuna) was one of its earliest pioneers, creating Budots music from his internet cafe in the mid 2000s.

The website visualizes Budots' rise in popularity over time. In August 2024, Budots went viral on TikTok, gaining new global attention.

Budots is rooted in the creativity of low-income Filipino communities, produced on pirated FL Studio software and blasted from street parties across the Philippines. Despite its cultural significance, Budots has often been dismissed due to classist attitudes and a broader bias towards Western artistic standards—ones which overlook accessible, grassroots expressions of joy, resilience, and innovation. Yet, Budots has already proven its mainstream appeal. Deeply infused with a DIY spirit, Budots challenges conventional definitions of art and is a testament to Filipino ingenuity.
